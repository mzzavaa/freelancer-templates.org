Turn quotes into shareable video content. Perfect for thought leadership, motivational content, and testimonial highlights.

## Use cases

- Inspirational quote posts
- Thought leadership content
- Testimonial highlights
- Book quote animations
- Podcast quote clips

## What you get

- **Centered** - quote centered with elegant typography
- **Bold** - large impactful text with accent colors
- **Minimal** - clean, simple design with subtle animation

## Formats

- **Square (1:1)** - Instagram feed, LinkedIn, Twitter
- **Vertical (9:16)** - Instagram Stories, TikTok
