Adapted from [reactvideoeditor/remotion-templates](https://github.com/reactvideoeditor/remotion-templates). The slide-text template is the simplest effect - a clean horizontal slide entrance with spring physics.

## Use cases

- Title cards
- Lower thirds
- Section transitions
- Simple text reveals

## What you get

- **Horizontal slide** - text slides in from right with spring damping
- **Fade in** - opacity animation combined with slide
- **Optional subtitle** - secondary text with delayed entrance
