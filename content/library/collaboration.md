Use for any announcement involving two parties. The split-screen format makes the partnership structure immediately legible.

## Use cases

- Agency + client partnership announcements
- Co-created product or course launches
- Team or company merger announcements
- Influencer x brand collab reveals

## What you get

- **Split Screen** - two-brand side-by-side with animated center merge
- **Announcement** - full-screen reveal with both logos and joint headline
- **Stats Merge** - combined metrics from both parties with animated counters
