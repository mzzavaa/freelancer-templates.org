An 8-second portrait reel (1080×1920) built for food creators, restaurant marketing, and dining review content. The hero food photo fills the frame; animated steam rises from the dish; a BlobShape adds organic warmth; ConfettiBurst fires at frame 80; and the dish name appears in WavyText. A price StickerBadge and restaurant handle cap the scene.

## Variants

**Warm Bistro** (TikTok vertical, THEME_WARM) — amber and rust tones, dark vignette overlay, warm-toned steam. Perfect for ramen, pizza, pasta, and comfort food content.

**Fresh Vibes** (Instagram Reels, THEME_FOREST) — clean greens and whites, minimal gradient, cool-toned steam. Perfect for salads, smoothie bowls, acai, and health food content.
