Use to make audio clips shareable on video-first platforms. The quote card variant works best for high-insight moments; waveform for musical/ambient intros.

## Use cases

- Instagram Reels/TikTok podcast clips
- Twitter/X audio quote cards
- YouTube Shorts from long-form podcast episodes
- Episode launch announcement content

## What you get

- **Waveform** - animated audio bars with episode info overlay
- **Quote Card** - highlighted quote with speaker attribution
- **Episode Promo** - full episode preview with title, guest, and CTA
