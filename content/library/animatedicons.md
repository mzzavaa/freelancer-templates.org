An 8-second portrait animation (1080×1920) that requires no stock photography — it's entirely procedural SVG. A large center icon pulses with glow rings while 6 supporting icons orbit it using trigonometric positioning. On the neon variant, a NeonGlowBorder frames the center. Title and tagline enter with a spring slide.

## Variants

**Neon Orbit** (THEME_NEON) — electric purple/cyan palette, glowing rings, NeonGlowBorder on the center, fast orbit at 25s period. Perfect for tech, SaaS, and digital product marketing.

**Minimal Flow** (THEME_MINIMAL) — crisp black/white, no glow, slow 40s orbit, line-weight icons. Perfect for portfolios, consulting, and process explainers.

## Use cases

- SaaS and app feature explainers
- Agency service overviews
- Tech stack "what I use" reels
- Onboarding or how-it-works animations
