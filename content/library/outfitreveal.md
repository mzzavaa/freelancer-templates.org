An 8-second portrait composition (1080×1920 · 240 frames) built around a single hero outfit shot with three layered playful elements:

- **WavyText** "FIT CHECK" — hero-size wavy headline that scales in with a bouncy spring
- **AnimatedSparkles** — 18 twinkling stars scattered across the frame, starting at frame 20
- **StickerBadge** — brand/price callout (e.g. `@brand $89`) that pops in with a rotated bouncy entrance at frame 38

## Visual variants

**BoldNeon** — dark overlay, hot pink sticker (`#ff6b9d`), gold sparkles (`#ffd700`). High-contrast, festival energy.

**PastelDream** — lighter overlay with a soft pink blush at the bottom, mint sticker (`#00cba9`), soft pink sparkles (`#ff9cc2`). Dreamy, editorial.

## Use cases

- Fashion creators doing "rate my fit" content
- OOTD (outfit of the day) posts with dramatic impact
- Festival fit reveals
- Brand partnership drops for clothing or accessories
