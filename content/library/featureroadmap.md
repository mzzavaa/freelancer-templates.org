Use in all-hands presentations, customer advisory boards, and investor updates.

## Use cases

- Quarterly product roadmap presentations
- Customer advisory board roadmap reveals
- Investor update product timeline slides
- Team planning session visual aids

## What you get

- **Timeline** - horizontal timeline with milestones and delivery dates
- **Swimlane** - team-per-lane roadmap with parallel workstreams
- **Grid** - quarter-by-quarter grid with initiative cards
