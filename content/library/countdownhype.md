Use in the 24-72 hours before a launch or deadline. Stack multiple teasers as the countdown reaches zero.

## Use cases

- Product launch countdown sequences
- Limited-time offer urgency cuts
- Event day-of countdown loops
- Sale end countdowns for e-commerce

## What you get

- **Timer** - large animated digit display with seconds ticking down
- **Teaser** - mystery reveal with partial info and countdown overlay
- **Urgency** - deadline-focused layout with scarcity messaging
