Adapted from [reactvideoeditor/remotion-templates](https://github.com/reactvideoeditor/remotion-templates). The original animated-text template was rebuilt with theme-aware colors and our TYPE scale. Characters animate individually with spring physics for natural motion.

## Use cases

- Brand name reveals
- Title cards and intro sequences
- Impact statements for social media
- Animated headings for presentation videos

## What you get

- **Per-character animation** - each letter drops in with rotation and fade
- **Spring physics** - natural bouncy motion, not linear easing
- **Optional subtitle** - fades in after the main text completes
