Use in early creative conversations to align on vision before execution. The mood board variant is best for visual-heavy concepts.

## Use cases

- Creative concept presentations
- Campaign arc pitches
- Brand direction proposals
- Video series concept reveals

## What you get

- **Arc** - narrative arc reveal with three-act structure
- **Board** - mood board grid with visual references and color palette
- **Brief** - structured creative brief with objectives and deliverables
