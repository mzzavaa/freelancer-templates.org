A 8-second portrait composition (1080×1920) with a cinematic 3-beat product reveal structure used by beauty and fashion brands:

## Beat structure

1. **Teaser (0–3s)** — blurred product image, "something new is coming" text whispers in. Mystery builds.
2. **Drop (3–5s)** — cuts to brand-color background with "NEW DROP" sticker badge animating in with a bounce spring + WavyText "LAUNCH" underneath.
3. **Hero (5–8s)** — product image slams in at full clarity, ConfettiBurst fires twice, product name and tagline animate up.

## Platform variants

**Luxury Black** — THEME_DARK (deep black, indigo accent), restrained animations, lower third captions. Suitable for fragrance, skincare, fashion house drops.

**Playful Pastel** — THEME_ROSE (rose/pink), energetic animations, full sparkles, TikTok upper third captions. Suitable for makeup, accessories, Gen-Z brands.

## Use cases

- Beauty brand new product launches
- Fashion drop announcements
- Limited edition capsule reveals
- Creator-led brand partnerships
