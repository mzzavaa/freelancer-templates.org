A 8-second portrait strip (1080×1920) that stacks four comic-style panels in a 2×2 grid. Each panel reveals with a spring-pop entrance, staggered 12 frames apart. A bold action word (BOOM! / POW!) blasts onto the center on a webkit-stroke burst, and a StickerBadge CTA slides in at the end.

## Variants

**Color Pop** — vivid THEME_BOLD palette, CMYK-inspired halftone dot grid background, SpeechBubble in accent yellow. High energy, TikTok-native feel.

**Noir Classic** — grayscale + red accent (THEME_MINIMAL), large-format halftone, inky black SpeechBubbles. Editorial comics meet cinema poster.

## Use cases

- Creator "hot take" panels
- Tutorial steps shown in comic format
- Behind-the-scenes moments narrated with speech bubbles
- Product comparisons styled as comic sketches
