Adapted from [reactvideoeditor/remotion-templates](https://github.com/reactvideoeditor/remotion-templates). The pulsing-text template was adapted with theme-colored glow and interpolation-based pulse timing.

## Use cases

- Music visualizer text
- Meditation and wellness content
- Ambient loop backgrounds
- Rhythmic branding elements

## What you get

- **Rhythmic pulse** - each character scales up and down on a cycle
- **Glow backdrop** - blurred accent circle behind each letter
- **Staggered timing** - characters pulse in sequence, not simultaneously
