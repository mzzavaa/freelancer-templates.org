Create engaging list content for Instagram Reels, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts. Perfect for "Top 5" lists, tips, and step-by-step guides.

## Use cases

- Top 5/10 lists for any topic
- Tips and tricks compilations
- Step-by-step tutorials
- Product feature highlights
- Educational content

## What you get

- **Stack** - items stack vertically with staggered entrance
- **Cards** - each item appears as a card with flip animation
- **Reveal** - progressive reveal with number counter

## Formats

- **Vertical (9:16)** - Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts
- **Square (1:1)** - Instagram feed, LinkedIn
