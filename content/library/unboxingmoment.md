An 8-second portrait composition (1080×1920) layered with maximum visual energy for unboxing and launch announcement content. Multiple elements fire sequentially to maintain momentum throughout.

## Layer stack (front to back)

1. **BlobShape × 3** — morphing organic blobs at corners and centre, creating a dynamic shifting background
2. **Product photo** — full-bleed with subtle zoom as energy builds
3. **ConfettiBurst × 3** — fires at frames 30, 90, 150 (1s, 3s, 5s) from different positions
4. **AnimatedSparkles × 2** — continuous throughout, secondary colour offset
5. **StickerBadge × 3** — product callouts bounce in at 0.7s, 2s, 4s
6. **WavyText hero** — product name with wave animation at bottom

## Style variants

**TikTok Hype** — THEME_NEON. Electric green accent, maximum confetti (50 particles per burst), 18 sparkles, `🔥` in copy, upper third safe zone. Peak energy.

**Reels Editorial** — THEME_DARK. Refined confetti (25 particles), 8 sparkles, professional copy, lower third safe zone. Still visually rich but controlled.

## Use cases

- Brand collab product unboxing reveals
- PR package opens for beauty/fashion
- Limited edition drop announcements
- Subscription box monthly reveal content
