A 9-second portrait reel (1080×1920) built for travel, wellness, and outdoor content. A full-bleed stock photo slowly pans using a Ken Burns effect. Animated leaves drift or clouds float in depending on the variant. Sparkles punctuate the scene, and a destination title plus hashtag fade in over a dark gradient.

## Variants

**Forest Morning** — THEME_FOREST greens, animated leaves fall across the frame with randomized sizes and rotation. Earthy overlay gradient. Ideal for hiking, forest bathing, and eco-living content.

**Ocean Sunset** — THEME_OCEAN blues, animated cloud drifts left-to-right, orange sparkles mimic sea glitter. Ideal for beach, travel, and golden hour reels.
