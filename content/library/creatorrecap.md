A ~7-second portrait montage (1080×1920 · 210 frames) showing a creator's week across 5 quick 1.2-second scenes, ending on a branded outro card:

| Scene | Stock category | Default label |
|-------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | festival | Day 1 |
| 2 | party | Day 2 |
| 3 | food | Day 3 |
| 4 | outfit | Day 4 |
| 5 | sunset | Day 5 |

Each scene shows a full-screen stock image with a **StickerBadge** ("DAY 1" … "DAY 5") and a platform-aware caption. The 30-frame outro ends on a **BlobShape** with the creator handle and a follow CTA.

## Platform variants

**TikTok** — upper-third bold-sans-stroke captions, THEME_BOLD purple/pink accent. Cut cadence matches TikTok's 30fps norm.

**Reels** — lower-third editorial captions with thin rule, THEME_DARK indigo accent. Matches Instagram editorial house style.

## Use cases

- Weekly vlog recaps compressed into a short-form reel
- Event or festival recap (swap stock images for real photos)
- Travel week summaries
- Content creators building a recognizable weekly format
