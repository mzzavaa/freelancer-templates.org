Use before or after running a poll - as a teaser to drive participation, or as a reveal after results come in.

## Use cases

- "Vote now" engagement reels before a community poll
- Survey results reveal content
- Quiz answer reveal sequences
- "Would you rather" engagement content

## What you get

- **Question Card** - animated poll question with answer options
- **Results Bar** - horizontal bar chart reveal showing vote percentages
- **Reveal** - dramatic answer reveal with animated percentage counter
