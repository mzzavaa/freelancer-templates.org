Showcase your metrics and achievements with animated number counters. Perfect for social proof and milestone celebrations.

## Use cases

- Milestone celebrations (followers, revenue, etc.)
- Year in review statistics
- Achievement highlights
- Business metrics showcase
- Growth updates

## What you get

- **Single** - one large animated counter with label
- **Grid** - multiple stats in a grid layout
- **Progress** - animated progress bar with percentage

## Formats

- **Square (1:1)** - Instagram feed, LinkedIn, Twitter
- **Vertical (9:16)** - Instagram Stories, TikTok
