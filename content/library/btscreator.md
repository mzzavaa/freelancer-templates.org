An 8-second portrait composition (1080×1920) with a vertical split-screen showing setup/before on the left and final/after on the right. The two panels slide in from opposite sides on cut, meeting at the centre with an animated divider line.

## Animation choreography

- **0–30f** — left (setup) panel slides in from off-screen left
- **15–45f** — right (final) panel slides in from off-screen right (offset = stagger effect)
- **25f** — centre divider scales down from top
- **30f** — BTS sticker badge bounces in centred on the divider
- **45–240f** — hold + ambient sparkles/blob shapes

## Style variants

**Chaotic Creator** — THEME_BOLD. Accent-coloured glowing centre line, blob shape behind sticker, full AnimatedSparkles. For content creators, makeup artists, fashion stylists.

**Clean Studio** — THEME_MINIMAL. Black divider, no sparkles, white/grey palette. For photographers, architects, product designers wanting a minimal professional feel.

## Use cases

- Beauty creators showing bare face vs. makeup look
- Set designers revealing setup vs. finished set
- Fashion stylists showing raw outfit vs. styled look
- Photographers comparing RAW vs. edited images
