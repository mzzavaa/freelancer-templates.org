Use in company all-hands, HR orientation, and internal communications.

## Use cases

- Global team distribution presentations
- New employee orientation content
- Regional team introduction videos
- Org chart alternative for distributed teams

## What you get

- **World View** - map showing office locations with pin animations
- **Card List** - employee cards with role and location
- **Region Groups** - team cards organized by geographic region
