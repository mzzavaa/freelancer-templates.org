An 11-second portrait reel (1080×1920) structured as 8 rapid 1-second location flashes followed by a 3-second end card. Built for travel recap reels and trip wrap-up content.

## Structure

- **8 × 30 frames** — location flashes. Each flash hard-cuts with a quick zoom-settle (Ken Burns on entry). A country emoji + city name sticker badge flies in on alternating corners.
- **90 frame end card** — final image fades in with sparkles. Large tally text ("8 CITIES · 14 DAYS") headlines the card. All visited cities list below as badge chips.

## Colour grades

**Film Grain** — THEME_ESPRESSO. Teal-orange split-tone colour overlay (teal shadows, warm highlights) for that desaturated, cinematic film grain aesthetic.

**Sunset Vibes** — THEME_SUNSET. Warm screen overlay lifts the highlights orange-pink. High saturation, golden hour mood throughout.

## Use cases

- Travel vloggers doing trip summary reels
- Travel agency promotional content
- Digital nomad "X countries in Y days" content
- Tourism brand campaigns
