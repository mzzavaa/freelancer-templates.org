An 8-second portrait composition (1080×1920) designed for inspirational quote content. The background image pans slowly using a Ken Burns horizontal+vertical drift, lending cinematic motion without any stock video.

## Design structure

- **Slow pan** — background image moves 3% horizontally and 2% vertically over 8 seconds. Creates motion without needing video footage.
- **Quote text** — large Merriweather/serif font, one emphasis word rendered as animated WavyText in the accent colour.
- **Editorial rules** — thin horizontal lines frame the quote above and below, fading in with the text.
- **Attribution** — italic, muted, smaller serif underneath.

## Style variants

**Minimal Cream** — THEME_CREAM. Light warm background tint, Merriweather serif, amber accent, zero-radius cards. Inspired by editorial print magazines.

**Dark Moody** — THEME_MIDNIGHT. Deep navy overlay, midnight blue accent, atmospheric sparkles. For motivational/nighttime aesthetic content.

## Use cases

- Motivational and mindset creators
- Book recommendation and literary accounts
- Brand values statements
- Mood board and aesthetic portfolio pieces
