Use starting 2-4 weeks before the event. Repurpose the countdown variant for day-of social stories.

## Use cases

- Conference and meetup announcement videos
- Webinar invite clips for email and social
- Speaker announcement reels
- Event recap teasers

## What you get

- **Hero** - date, location, and headline with dramatic entrance animation
- **Speakers** - speaker card grid with names, titles, and company
- **Countdown** - live countdown timer with event details
