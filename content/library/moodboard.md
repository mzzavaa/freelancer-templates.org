A 9-second portrait video (1080×1920) that presents a 3×3 grid of lifestyle images. Each tile spring-pops in with a staggered 8-frame offset. Every 30 frames, a NeonGlowBorder cycles to the next tile, subtly zooming it to 1.04× scale. A title and StickerBadge fade up from the bottom over a gradient.

## Variants

**Vibrant Mix** (THEME_BOLD) — colorful palette, saturated image filters, rounded tiles, glowing neon borders. Great for trend reports, "what I'm obsessed with" content, and creator mood dumps.

**Editorial Grid** (THEME_CREAM) — clean cream background, grayscale-boosted images, sharp 2px black borders, serif title. Great for fashion mood boards, brand aesthetic reels, and editorial showcases.

## Use cases

- "Current obsessions" content series
- Brand mood board presentations
- Weekly picks or favorites reels
- Fashion and lifestyle editorial content
