## Use cases

- Ambient background loops
- Music and audio content backgrounds
- Wellness and meditation videos
- Abstract visual transitions

## What you get

- **SVG wave path** - sine-wave computed per frame for smooth motion
- **Gradient fill** - uses theme accent -> accentSecondary gradient
- **Text overlay** - optional centered text with glow shadow
