## The Problem

A lead reaches out. You have a great first call. You say you will follow up next week. Life gets busy, a project runs long, and three weeks later you remember that lead - too late. They hired someone else.

Leads fall through the gaps not because you are disorganized, but because you are managing your pipeline in your head. There is no system reminding you to follow up, no record of what was discussed, no visibility into where each deal actually stands.

Enterprise CRM tools cost hundreds per month and are built for sales teams, not solo operators. You end up with nothing - or a spreadsheet you stop updating within a week.

## The Solution

A Notion-based CRM built for the way freelancers actually work. Each lead has a card with status, last contact date, next action, and notes. A view shows you what needs attention today. Follow-up reminders surface automatically without you having to check anything.

The pipeline moves from lead to proposal to contract to project - the same Notion workspace that runs the rest of your business. No additional software. No monthly fee. No learning curve.
