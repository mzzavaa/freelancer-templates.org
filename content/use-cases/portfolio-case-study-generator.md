## The Problem

You finish a great project. The work is strong. The results are real. But you never write up the case study - there is no time, and it feels like starting from scratch. The project closes and the evidence of your best work disappears into a folder.

Six months later you need to pitch a similar client and realize you have nothing to show. You have done this exact project before. You just never documented it.

Portfolio building consistently falls to the bottom of the priority list because it feels like extra work done after the work is done. Every freelancer says they will do it next week. Most never do.

## The Solution

At project close, a case study generates from the project data you already captured during the engagement - the brief, deliverables, timeline, and results. The structure is fixed: challenge, approach, outcome. You fill in a few specific details and it is done.

For visual work, a Remotion video case study reel takes the same data and produces a short, shareable video - a project recap that works on LinkedIn, in proposals, and on your portfolio site. You build your portfolio while you work, not instead of it.
