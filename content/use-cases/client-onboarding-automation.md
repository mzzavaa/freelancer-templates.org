## The Problem

A client says yes. Now you have 2-3 hours of setup work ahead of you. Write a personalized welcome email. Draft and send the contract. Set up the project folder. Build a Notion workspace from scratch. Create a kickoff agenda. Send calendar invites.

Every step is manual, every new client starts from zero. If you are onboarding 2-3 new clients a month, that is a full day of admin before any real work starts.

The other cost is inconsistency. When you build onboarding manually each time, clients get different levels of professionalism. Some get a polished welcome email, others get a rushed one. Some get a complete Notion workspace, others get a folder with a few files. Your brand looks different every time.

## The Solution

A single trigger - contract signed - kicks off a structured onboarding sequence. A welcome email goes out automatically. The project workspace duplicates from a template. The kickoff meeting agenda populates with the right questions. Everything is in place before you have even opened your laptop.

The client experience is consistent and professional every time. You spend your first interaction doing actual work, not setup.
